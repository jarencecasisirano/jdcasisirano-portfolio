About Me

I am Jarence. I graduated from the University of the Philippines Diliman in Quezon City with a Bachelor's Degree in Geodetic Engineering. My core expertise is in mapping, geospatial analysis, and remote sensing. Because of this background, I am able to handle a wide range of geospatial tasks. I am familiar and comfortable in handling geospatial data (including earth observation data; e.g., satellite imagery), manipulating them, and performing analysis to generate and derive actionable insights. I am well-versed in using various geospatial software, such as ArcGIS, QGIS, and Google Earth Engine, to create and visualize geospatial data through maps.

Currently, I am taking up my Master's Degree in Geomatics specializing in Remote Sensing and Photogrammetry at the University of the Philippines Diliman while working full-time as a researcher at the same university. My current interests also include systems development, spatial data science, and automation. I am comfortable in using Python and R for geospatial analysis. I am strongly inclined to use my skills in the field of environmental monitoring and conservation.

Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I try a variety of things. Some of my favorite hobbies include watching animated series and playing video games. I also enjoy playing sports and music.
